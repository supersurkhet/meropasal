Welcome to MeroPasal — मेरो पसल — the complete retail management platform built for Nepal.

Let me walk you through everything this app can do for your business.

Starting with the landing page — you can see our beautiful hero section with both English and Nepali text. The app supports full bilingual operation. Click "Get Started" to begin.

Now we're at the WorkOS login page. You can sign in with Google, GitHub, Microsoft, or Apple. Let's log in with GitHub.

After logging in for the first time, you'll see the onboarding wizard. This is where you set up your business. Step one — enter your business name and select your type: Retail, Wholesale, or Service. Notice the Nepali labels — खुद्रा, थोक, सेवा.

Step two — add your location, phone number, and PAN number. Step three — set your fiscal year in Bikram Sambat format. The app auto-detects the current Nepali fiscal year.

Click "Complete Setup" and you're taken to your dashboard. This is your business command center — showing revenue, expenses, outstanding payments, and low stock alerts at a glance.

Let's explore the modules. First, Parties — these are your suppliers. Click "New Party" to add a supplier. Fill in the name, PAN number, phone, address, and credit limit. Click "Create Party" and it appears in your list instantly — real-time updates powered by Convex.

Next, Customers — same clean interface. Add your buyers with their contact details and email.

Now Products — this is where the smart automation shines. When you enter a cost price, the selling price auto-fills at cost plus ten percent. The reorder level auto-calculates based on your opening stock. You can set compound units like "box of 12" and the app handles piece-level pricing automatically.

Stock Import is how you record purchases from suppliers. Select your supplier, and the product list automatically filters to show only that supplier's products. Add items, quantities, and the unit prices auto-fill from your cost prices. When you submit, the app automatically creates a purchase invoice and updates your stock book — zero manual work.

For Sales, select a customer, add products, and the app shows available stock right next to the quantity field. Green means plenty in stock, yellow means running low, red means you're about to run out. The app prevents you from selling more than what's available.

Orders support partial payments. Add items, record payments as they come in — cash, card, bank transfer, online, or check. The payment status updates automatically — pending, partial, or paid. When you mark an order as done, the app creates the invoice and updates stock automatically.

Invoices are generated automatically — you never create them manually. Every purchase, sale, order completion, and trip return creates an invoice. You can view, print, and customize the bill template in Settings.

The Stock Book is your complete inventory audit trail. See available stock per product, broken down by supplier bucket. Run a fiscal year close to carry forward balances.

The Ledger shows double-entry bookkeeping records. Every transaction creates balanced debit and credit entries. Check the trial balance to verify everything adds up.

For logistics, manage your Vehicles and create Trips. Dispatch products on a truck, and when the trip returns, enter what came back. The app calculates what was sold and auto-creates a sale invoice.

Reports give you three views — Sales Report with trends and top products, Inventory Report with stock levels and low-stock alerts, and Financial Report with profit and loss, receivables and payables.

In Settings, customize your organization details and your bill template. The Bill Template Editor has a live preview — change columns, paper size, font, add your logo, and see the result instantly.

Now watch this — click the language switcher. Everything switches to Nepali. ड्यासबोर्ड, आपूर्तिकर्ता, ग्राहक, सामान — every label, every button, every description. And toggle dark mode for comfortable nighttime use.

MeroPasal also supports desktop and mobile apps. Download the Windows app built with Tauri, or the Android app built with Capacitor. Your staff can log in and manage the business from anywhere.

That's MeroPasal — inventory, invoicing, stock book, logistics, and accounting — all in one place, built with care for Nepali businesses. Get started free at meropasal.com.
